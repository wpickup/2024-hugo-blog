Ceramics Destinations in Kyoto

The following are worth considering if you are in Kyoto and interested in Ceramics. Kawai Kanjiros’ House We have visited this beautiful old merchants house with its huge climbing kiln several times and always found it rewarding. The enormous climbing kiln and beautifully preserved house are worth spending some time exploring. Kawai Kanjiro was a key player in Soetsu Yanangi’s “Mingei” movement1 in partnership Shoji Hamada, Kenichi Tomimoto and Bernard Leach.
